Technical Events

Acharya Nagarjuna University is proud to host a series of technical events that bring together students, faculty, and industry experts to showcase innovative ideas, share knowledge, and foster collaboration. Our technical events are designed to provide a platform for students to demonstrate their skills, learn from industry leaders, and network with like-minded individuals.

  • Develops technical skills: Our technical events help students develop practical skills and expertise in various technologies and domains.
  • Enhances networking opportunities: Students connect with industry experts, faculty, and peers, expanding their professional network and potential job opportunities

Cultural Events

Acharya Nagarjuna University is proud to host a vibrant array of cultural events that showcase the diversity and richness of our college community. Our cultural events are designed to promote cross-cultural understanding, appreciation, and exchange, and to provide a platform for students to express themselves and share their cultural heritage.

  • Festivals: We celebrate various festivals and holidays from around the world, such as Diwali, Chinese New Year, and Christmas, with traditional music, dance, food, and decorations.
  • Cultural Shows: Students perform traditional dances, songs, and plays that showcase their cultural heritage and talent.

Sports Events

Acharya Nagarjuna University is excited to announce a thrilling lineup of sports events that will showcase the athletic talent and competitive spirit of our students. Our sports events are designed to promote physical fitness, teamwork, and sportsmanship, while providing an entertaining experience for participants and spectators alike.

  • Promotes physical fitness: Our sports events encourage students to engage in regular physical activity, improving their overall health and well-being.
  • Enhances college life: Our sports events add to the vibrancy and excitement of college life, creating lasting memories and experiences for our students.
